How It Works

The Parsing Pipeline


1
Feed Ingestion
Raw social media posts are collected from APIs (Twitter, Instagram, Reddit) or uploaded as text batches.
2
Tokenization & Cleaning
Text is tokenized into words. URLs, special chars, and stop words are filtered out for cleaner analysis.
3
Entity Extraction
Hashtags (#tags), mentions (@users), URLs and keywords are identified using regex pattern matching.
4
Sentiment Classification
Each post is scored Positive, Negative, or Neutral using lexicon-based keyword matching and scoring algorithms.
